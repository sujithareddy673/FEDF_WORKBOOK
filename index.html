Animated Demo

Subtle, modern animated webpage

A responsive, single-file example with floating backgrounds, animated cards, and subtle micro-interactions you can adapt for demos or prototypes.

Fluid animations CSS-driven, GPU-friendly keyframes and transform-based motion.
2
Responsive Adapts across screen sizes with simple grid and flex layouts.
3

4
Composable Drop-in blobs, gradient, and cards — customize colors and timing.
